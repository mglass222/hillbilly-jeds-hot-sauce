Our Story

From Jed's Porch to Your Plate.

My name's Jeff Briggs — though most folks round here just call me Jed. I started bottlin' hot sauce on my back porch in 2019 with peppers from my momma's garden and a recipe that got passed down further than I can remember.

Word got 'round. First it was neighbors. Then it was the feed store. Now we ship jars all over the country, but every bottle still gets capped right here in North Carolina — same porch, same pot, same stubborn old dog beggin' for scraps.

— Jeff "Jed" Briggs
